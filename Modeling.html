Modeling

Our project to produce vitamin A and flavor by Saccharomyces Cerevisiae on space thank to a co culture with Clostridium Ljungdahlii rapidly raise complex issues. In particular, in terms of yield, flux and feasibility of our system. Naturally one way to answer to these question was to modelling our system.

Constraints

The first step, before to ask anything to our models, is to resume all our constraints of our systems that will be applied to our models. So, in a space culture we get few constraints :

  • No carbohydrates as carbon source
  • CO2 as carbon source
  • H2 as energy source
  • A minimum culture medium with the least compound

Questioning

The questioning plan of our system can be decomposed by different questions, which must be the simplest, in order to have a precise answer.

  1. What is the most suitable minimal culture medium within constraints ?
    Our project is in a spaceship and the resources are very limited so we must know the minimum medium that we need to make our coculture work.

  2. How much carbon products can C. ljungdahlii make?
    Which is the theoretical maximum yield of acetate and ethanol in function of μ? In order to know if the production of carbon source of C. ljungdahlii is enough to allow S. cerevisiae to growth.
